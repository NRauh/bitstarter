The Friendly Leaf

the perfect pressed leaf

Bring autumn into your life, any time of the year with the Friendly Leaf. Our Friendly Leaves can fill that void in your life, and even make the perfect gift for loved ones and friends. Fresh from the midwestern state of Iowa, your leaf will be handpicked, and pressed the day the the goal is met (or order is received if the goal is met), and mailed to you soon after.

For a few cents more you can have sentience added to your leaf, with our fantastic goggly-eyes. Have your Friendly Leaf become your new companion. They're small enough to come with you anywhere, and entertaining enough for you to want to bring them along too. Think of the adventures you and your leaf would go on! You can even play dress up with leaf, which is a perfect way to entertain the young and young at heart. It can even entertain pets!
